C-LOOK: Circular LOOK Algorithm

The C-LOOK disk scheduling algorithm is a modified version of LOOK that moves only between the lowest and highest request, skipping unnecessary movements. It provides better performance compared to LOOK.

Advantages:

• No starvation.

• More efficient than LOOK and SCAN.

• Reduces unnecessary movement to extreme ends.

Disadvantages:

• Overhead in determining the last request.

• More computational complexity than LOOK.
