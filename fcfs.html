FCFS: First Come First Serve

FCFS (First Come First Serve) is the simplest disk scheduling algorithm. Each request is taken/executed in the sequence of its arrival in the disk queue. As a result, there is no starvation in this algorithm. However, it isn't fast compared to other algorithms.

Advantages:

• No starvation in FCFS.

• Simple and straightforward approach.

• Fair chance is given to each request.

Disadvantages:

• Not an optimized approach.

• Can be time-consuming.
